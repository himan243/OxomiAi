# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - OxomiAi

## 1. Project Overview
**OxomiAi** is a district-wise interactive cultural discovery platform for Assam. It aims to empower local communities to document and share their cultural heritage, shifting tourism from popular spots to overlooked rural areas, thereby creating local economic opportunities.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Tourists:** Seeking authentic cultural experiences, festivals, and hidden gems.
*   **Local Communities:** Residents wanting to showcase their traditions, food, and crafts.
*   **Administrators:** Moderating content to ensure quality and authenticity.

## 3. Core Features
### 3.1 Interactive Assam Map
*   Visual representation of Assam with 30+ clickable districts.
*   Direct navigation to district-specific cultural pages.

### 3.2 District Cultural Pages
*   Categorized content: Festivals, Food, Craft, Folk Art, Heritage, Events, Hidden Spots.
*   Media galleries (Photos/Videos).
*   Dynamic filtering by tags.

### 3.3 Community Contribution System
*   Submission form for local users.
*   Auto-tagging by district.
*   Pending queue for moderation.

### 3.4 Admin Panel
*   Dashboard for approving/rejecting submissions.
*   Content management (Edit/Delete).
*   Tag and district management.

### 3.5 Tourist Exploration & Itinerary
*   Saved items list (Personal Itinerary).
*   Search and filter capabilities.

## 4. UI/UX Goals
*   **Cultural Aesthetic:** Incorporate traditional Assamese patterns and warm colors.
*   **Mobile-First:** Seamless experience on smartphones.
*   **Visual-Heavy:** Large, high-quality image/video galleries.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   Number of community submissions.
*   Breadth of coverage across all Assam districts.
*   User engagement with the itinerary feature.
