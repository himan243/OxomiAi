# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - OxomiAi

## 1. Project Overview
**OxomiAi** is a digital sanctuary for Assam's diverse cultural heritage. It serves as an interactive platform for discovering, documenting, and preserving the traditions, festivals, crafts, and lore of all districts in Assam. By empowering local communities to share their stories, OxomiAi aims to bridge the gap between popular tourism and overlooked rural treasures, creating a sustainable ecosystem for cultural preservation.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Cultural Explorers & Tourists:** Seeking authentic, off-the-beaten-path experiences, local festivals, and traditional crafts.
*   **Local Communities:** Residents, artisans, and elders who want to document and showcase their heritage to the world.
*   **Content Creators:** Individuals passionate about storytelling and cultural documentation.
*   **Administrators:** Curators ensuring the quality, authenticity, and safety of the community-contributed content.

## 3. Core Features

### 3.1 Interactive Cultural Map (Assam)
*   A stylized, interactive SVG/Leaflet map of Assam with all districts clearly delineated.
*   Visual feedback on hover and click for each district.
*   Direct navigation to district-specific cultural hubs.

### 3.2 District Cultural Hubs
*   Richly visual pages for each district displaying curated content.
*   Categorization: Festivals, Food, Craft, Folk Art, Heritage, and "Hidden Gems."
*   Media-centric layout with high-quality images and videos.

### 3.3 Content Library & Global Search
*   A centralized repository (Library) for exploring all cultural content across Assam.
*   Global search and advanced filtering by district, category, and media type.

### 3.4 Community Contribution & Co-Creation
*   **Submission System:** Public form for users to upload stories, photos, and videos.
*   **Content Suggestions:** Ability for users to suggest edits or improvements to existing cultural entries.
*   **Media Support:** Integration with cloud storage (Supabase) for large-scale media hosting.

### 3.5 Personal Journey (Itinerary)
*   A "Journey" feature allowing users to save districts or specific cultural spots to their personal exploration list.
*   Persistent storage for user-curated itineraries.

### 3.6 Admin Moderation Dashboard
*   Protected portal for reviewing community submissions.
*   Workflow for approving, rejecting, or editing pending content before it goes public.

## 4. UI/UX Vision
*   **Aesthetic:** "Modern Traditionalist" — combining clean, high-performance web standards with textures, patterns, and colors inspired by Assamese textiles (Gamosa, Muga silk) and nature.
*   **Experience:** Immersive and cinematic, utilizing fluid animations (Framer Motion) and a responsive, mobile-first design.
*   **Accessibility:** Fast-loading images and intuitive navigation for diverse age groups.

## 5. Success Metrics
*   **Breadth of Coverage:** Content representing all 30+ districts of Assam.
*   **Community Growth:** Increasing number of verified community submissions and suggestions.
*   **Retention:** Repeat visits to the Library and personal Itinerary features.
